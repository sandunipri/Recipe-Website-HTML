Espresso Coffee

To make espresso, grind 18-20 grams of coffee to a fine consistency, similar to table salt. Tamp the grounds firmly into the portafilter, creating an even surface. Preheat your espresso machine, then brew the shot for 25-30 seconds, producing about 1.5-2 ounces of espresso with a layer of crema on top. Enjoy it as is or use it as a base for other coffee drinks.
