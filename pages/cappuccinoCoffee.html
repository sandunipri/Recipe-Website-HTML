CAPPUCCINO COFFEE INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tablespoons finely ground dark roast coffee
  • 4 ounces water
  • 4 ounces milk

Description: Known for its equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam, the cappuccino offers a balanced, rich flavor and a thick, foamy texture.

Tips: Use foamy, airy milk for the top layer to contrast with the smooth espresso. You can sprinkle a little cocoa powder or cinnamon on top for added flavor.

"A cappuccino is a classic espresso-based coffee drink that consists of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, resulting in a balanced, creamy, and velvety texture. Originating in Italy, cappuccinos are known for their rich, bold coffee flavor combined with the sweetness and smoothness of the milk. Typically served in a small cup, a cappuccino has a stronger espresso taste than a latte due to its smaller milk content, and the foam layer provides a satisfying mouthfeel. Many baristas top it with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon for added aroma and flavor.
