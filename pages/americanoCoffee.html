INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ounces (2 shots) espresso
  • 4 ounces hot water

Description: A smooth, mellow coffee with the boldness of espresso but less intensity, resembling traditional drip coffee.

Tips: Add hot water slowly to avoid diluting the crema too much. For a stronger Americano, use a double shot of espresso.

" An Americano is a popular espresso-based coffee that combines one or two shots of espresso with hot water, creating a drink that's similar in strength to drip coffee but with a distinctively rich, smooth taste. This method preserves the bold, slightly caramelized flavor of espresso while mellowing its intensity and bitterness with the addition of water. The Americano originated during World War II, when American soldiers in Europe would dilute espresso with hot water to make it resemble the coffee they were used to back home.
