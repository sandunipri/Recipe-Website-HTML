COLD BREW COFFEE INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup (about 85 grams) of coarsely ground coffee beans
  • 4 cups (950 ml) of cold, filtered water
  • Ice (for serving)
  • Ice (for serving)

Description: Smooth, mild, and naturally sweet. Cold brewing reduces acidity, making this drink refreshing and easy to sip.

Tips: Use a coarse grind and a 12-24 hour brew time for a perfect balance. Serve over ice with milk or sweetener as desired.

" Cold brew is a smooth, refreshing coffee made by steeping coarse coffee grounds in cold water for an extended period, usually 12-24 hours. Unlike iced coffee, which is brewed hot and then chilled, cold brew is never heated, less acidic taste profile due to the slow extraction process. This method produces a concentrated coffee that can be diluted with water, milk, or even flavored syrups to customize its strength and flavor.
